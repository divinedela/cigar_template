Corto

Short and full bodied, a first from Warped.

BRAND STORY

Corto was the first full bodied blend we crafted, but full bodied in our eyes. A complex cigar with a depth of body but not over powering. Designed for time sensitive cigar moments.

CIGAR SIZE

X46
4.5 x 46 in   •   Full Body   •   Box of 25

The Corto X46 is comprised of a Nicaraguan Corojo ’99 wrapper, Nicaragua Coroijo ’99 and Criollo ’98 fillers, and Nicaraguan binders. Offering a full bodied experience with notes of black pepper, dense wood, leather, chocolate, caramel, and molasses.
